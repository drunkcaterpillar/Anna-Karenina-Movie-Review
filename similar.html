Anna Karenina is set in a time that is the height of what is now called the Golden Age of Russian Literature.

Romanticism was at its peak, and the movement inspired all genres.

The movies based on this time have lush visuals and elegant characters. One can pause at any scene in Anna Karenina and it would look like a beautiful photograph.

If one loves period dramas, the way they dress and how everything is decorated in intricate ways, the movie Atonement will be a perfect fit to watch after Anna Karenina, especially because it is very influential in terms of style and psychological complexity as well.

Another movie would be Never Let Me Go, which also explores the inner ranges of human experience, trying to capture individual struggle.
